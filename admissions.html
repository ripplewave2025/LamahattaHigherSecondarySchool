Admissions

Enroll at Lamahatta HSS

Information, requirements, and key steps for families seeking to join our learning community.

How to Apply
01

1. Form Collection

Pick up the official application form from the school administrative office during office hours.

02

2. Document Preparation

Assemble all required certificates, identity copies, and passport-size photographs.

03

3. Submission & Review

Submit the completed form to the office for verification of age eligibility and subject combinations.

04

4. Confirmation

Once verified, finalize the registration by paying the nominal government fees and collecting your identity card.

Required Documents Checklist

  • Original and photocopy of Birth Certificate.
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous recognized school.
  • Mark sheet and Admit Card of the qualifying examination.
  • Aadhar Card copy of the student and parent.
  • Three recent passport-size colored photographs.
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC), if applicable.
