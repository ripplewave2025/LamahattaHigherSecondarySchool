Academics

Our Curricular Framework

Rigorous academic pathways designed to prepare students for higher studies and professional life.

V – X

Secondary Division (Classes V - X)

Following the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) curriculum, we focus on fundamental sciences, languages, mathematics, and social sciences to establish a strong analytical base.

XI – XII

Higher Secondary Division (Classes XI - XII)

Affiliated with the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE), we currently offer a comprehensive Arts stream designed to build critical thinking and research skills.

Higher Secondary Stream (Arts)

For Higher Secondary students, the primary medium of learning and first language is Nepali, with English served as the second language. Subject combinations include:

Political Science History Geography Economics

Future Expansion Vision

Plans to introduce the Science stream and additional vocational combinations are currently under active development. The school administration is expanding resources and facility infrastructure to support this vision.
