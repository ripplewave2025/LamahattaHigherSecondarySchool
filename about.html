Our Profile

Serving the Darjeeling hills since 1964

Lamahatta Higher Secondary School stands as an institution of learning, discipline, and regional progress, nestled in Upper Lamahatta.

Our Historical Journey

Estd. 1964 - Foundation

Founded as a primary learning center to serve Upper Lamahatta and neighboring mountain Busties.

1969 - Class X Recognition

Officially recognized up to Class X by the West Bengal Board, becoming a vital secondary school in the district.

2000 - Higher Secondary Upgrade

Upgraded to Higher Secondary status, offering senior streams to help students transition to college and career paths.
